6. Escrow Services

The PayLobsterEscrow smart contract (0xa091fC821c85Dfd2b2B3EF9e22c5f4c8B8A24525) provides trustless escrow functionality:

  • Creation: USDC is locked in the smart contract until release conditions are met
  • Release: Only the buyer can release funds to the seller
  • Refund: Only the seller can refund funds to the buyer (or buyer after deadline)
  • Disputes: Either party can flag an escrow as disputed
  • Arbitration: The designated arbiter can resolve disputes

Important: Escrow transactions are irreversible once executed on-chain. We cannot recover funds sent to incorrect addresses or reverse completed transactions.
